About me

Hello! I’m Masopha Phooko, a professional photographer based in Maseru. With over a decade of experience behind the lens, I specialize in capturing the beauty of everyday life. From the grandeur of cityscapes to the intimate moments of portraiture, my work is a celebration of the world around us.

Photography for me is not just a profession, but a passion. It’s about seeing the extraordinary in the ordinary, and finding the magic in the mundane. I believe that every picture tells a story, and I strive to tell those stories in the most compelling way possible.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope my work resonates with you and I look forward to the possibility of working together
